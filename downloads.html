Homestead Planner

macOS · version 0.1.0 · early test build

A planning tool for small farms and homesteads — track plantings, beds, and harvests through the season. Pick the build that matches your Mac.

Not sure which one? Click the Apple menu → About This Mac. If it says "Apple M1/M2/M3," choose Apple Silicon. If it says "Intel," choose Intel Mac.

These builds aren't notarized yet, so on first launch macOS may warn that the app is from an unidentified developer. Right-click the app and choose Open to run it anyway.
